GM

Game Master

GM refers to the person organizing the game. Generally speaking, this is the person in charge of making decision about in-game events as they relate to situations where the game rules are not clearly defined. Anyone can do this, but having more game experience certainly makes this easier

PC

Player Character

These are the characters within the stories we play. Always remember that - even though we are roleplaying make-belief - there is a real person with real feelings behind the character.
