Gameplay Terminology Status Effects Character Creation Abilities

GM

Game Master

This is the person organizing the game. Generally speaking, the GM is in charge of making decision about in-game events as they relate to situations where the game rules are not clearly defined.

PC

Player Character

These are the characters our players play. Always remember that - even though we are roleplaying make-belief - there is a real person with real feelings behind the character.

NPC

Non-Player Character

These are the characters the GM plays. They may be shopkeepers, passers-by, or enemies, and are typically only played (or even created) as the PCs meet and interact with them.

BBEG

Big Bad Evil Guy

A final boss-type character which the PCs fight, usually met near the end of a campaign, or at the culmination of a significant story arc.

Lv

Level

A level is a general measure of strength. Character levels start at 1, and while there is no theoretical upper limit, it is very uncommon to move past Lv20.

HP

Health Points

A representation of PC durability. The more HP you have, the longer you can stay in dangerous situations without falling.

MP

Mana Points

Mana is used to perform spells and other abilities. Such abilities are usually significantly stronger than basic (non-Mana) abilities.

AP

Attribute Points

Attributes are numerical descriptors of a character's capabilities. There are 6 attributes: Constitution, Strength, Dexterity, Intelligence, Wisdom and Charisma.

CON

Constitution

Constitution refers to a character's physical resilience. It directly affects maxHP, physical Resistance (PR), as well as their Endurance and Resistance ability rolls.

STR

Strength

Strength is a mesaure of a character's ability to apply brute force using their body. It also directly increases Melee Damage (MD), as well as Athletics, Grappling and Intimidation ability rolls.

DEX

Dexterity

Dexterity could also be called agility. It affects a character's ability to dodge incoming attacks (DV), as well as Acrobatics, Reflex, Stealth and Thievery ability rolls.

INT

Intelligence

Intelligence refers to a character's resourcefulness. It directly affects maxMana, as well as the ability rolls for Crafting, History, Investigation and Mindfulness rolls.

WIS

Wisdom

Wisdom is a measure of how in-tune a character is with their surroundings, including the winds of magic. Ability rolls in this category are Intuition, Perception, Survival and Willpower.

CHA

Charisma

Charisma is the art of wordplay and acting. It is the most influential role-play attribute for non-combat encounters, and also affects Inspiration, Leadership, Performance and Persuasion Rolls.

BP

Background Points

BP are powerful points that very heavily influence how your PC plays. They are rare and should be spent very deliberately. These points allow almost limitless customizability for your PCs.

SP

Skill Points

Skill Points are relatively more common and less powerful points, but still allow high player freedom in their PC creation. Please refer to the ability guide when creating these.
