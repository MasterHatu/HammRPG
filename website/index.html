Welcome to the HammRPG homepage

HammRPG is a tabletop roleplaying game (TTRPG) loosely in the style of other famous TTRPGs you may already have heard of. It began because a small group of friends wanted to try out the collective story-telling experience but could not afford to buy any official rule books. Instead, we decided to create a brand-new system, which is what you see here today.

Because of this background, the base ruleset of HammRPG is and always will be completely free to use. It does take a lot of time and effort to create and maintain a system like this, so if you feel like contributing, please visit our Patreon Page

The system is designed to function within a wide range of universes, allowing for as much freedom for player creativity as possible. Rather than picking from a long list of possible abilities and features, each player custom creates their own abilities from scratch. That being said, in order to achieve some form of balance between the different players, we do use a few general rules to guide you as you create your abilities.

For simplicity's sake, we have based all of our games so far loosely on the existing Warhammer fantasy universe created by Games Workshop; however, be aware that there is no official affiliation between HammRPG and Games Workshop. All content provided here should be considered as a work of fanfiction only.

Head over to the Gameplay section to familiarize yourself with the ruleset we use to play, and then check out the Character Creation section to start building your very own character right away. The next time a Game Master creates a new game, you might be one of the players to participate in writing the story!

If you prefer not to be a player yourself, you can still enjoy HammRPG by reading read along as we play one of the ongoing stories, or just head to the archive section and read stories that have already been completed.

We hope you enjoy the game.
